Hax List:


To submit a hack:
Record yourself doing the hack.
Click the report a bug button in-game and select hack.
Mention any useful information including the Youtube link.

Dupe item:

Found By:
Dupe a specific tradable valuable item as many times as wanted.
Reward: Keep 1-100 copy of the item. (Amount depends on value)

Dupe equip:

Found By:
Dupe an equip which was generated by a player.
Reward: Keep a copy of the item.

PvP Bonus outside:

Found By:
Leave the PvP Zone and keep either the boost or abilities for more than 30 seconds.
Reward: Keep

PvP On outside:

Found By:
Leave PvP and still being able to attack players for more than 30 seconds.
Reward: Able to attack any players for 1 day.

Quest completions:

Found By:
Trigger a Quest completion multiple times without having to do the tasks again.
Reward: Get 0.95% reward tier for that quest. (Will not appear on highscore though.)

Skilling plot:

Found By:
Harvest same Skilling Plot multiple times without having to complete the quest.
Reward: Exp and items equivalent to 100 harvestings.
